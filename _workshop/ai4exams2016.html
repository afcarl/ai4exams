AI4Exams 2016
April 7-8, 2016
Somewhere in Tokyo
TBA


Artificial Intelligence for Exam Question Answering (AI4Exams) is a 2-day meeting held at The National Institute of Informatics, Tokyo, from April 7th to 8th. The goal of the workshop is to increase international awareness of on-going research into exam answering / QA systems, and to formalize the task (data and tools) moving forward.

Please contact the organizers for more information or to register. There is no registration fee, and breakfast and lunch will be provided.
