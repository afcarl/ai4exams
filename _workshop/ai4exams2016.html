Artificial Intelligence for Exam Question Answering (AI4Exams) is a 2-day meeting held at The National Institute of Informatics (NII), Tokyo, from June 2nd to 3rd. The goal of the workshop is to increase international awareness of on-going research into exam answering / QA systems, and to formalize the task (data and tools) to improve the quality of research moving forward.
